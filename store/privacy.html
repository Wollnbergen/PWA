Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Sultan Wallet ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes how Sultan Wallet handles information for our browser extension.

1. No Personal Information Collection

Sultan Wallet is a non-custodial digital asset wallet. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information, including:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Your IP address or device information
  • Your private keys, recovery phrases, or passwords

2. Local Storage Only

All sensitive information, such as your private keys and wallet data, is stored locally on your device and encrypted. We never transmit this data to our servers or any third parties.

3. Transaction Data

When you perform transactions on the Sultan network, the transaction data is broadcast to the public blockchain. This data is public by nature and is not controlled by Sultan Wallet.

4. Third-Party Services

Sultan Wallet may interact with third-party RPC providers or dApps. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sltn.io.
